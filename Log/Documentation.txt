################################################################################
#	Inns
################################################################################

Generated random inns have:
* Name
* Innkeeper
* Rumors
* Accommodations

################################################################################
#	Rumor 
################################################################################

Randomly generated rumors will have the structure:
Actors + Action 

Actors are the people or items that the rumor is describing.
Actions decribe either what the players must do about the actors or what the actors are doing.

When adding to the rumor generator, add to .txt files:
	rumor_actions.txt
	rumor_actors.txt
	rumor_grammar.txt

rumor_grammar contains pairs in the format: singular - plural
If necessary, tweak the singular_keys list in genRumor.py to help identify actors as singular.
